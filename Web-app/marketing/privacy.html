Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Welcome to FormatX. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our service:

  • Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture via OAuth 2.0. We do not store your Google password.
  • Google Docs Access: With your permission, we access only the specific Google Doc you choose to format. We request the minimum OAuth scopes necessary.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymised usage metrics (e.g., number of formats, templates used) to improve our service.
  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record your IP address, browser type, and pages visited for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the FormatX service.
  • To format your Google Docs according to the template you select.
  • To improve, personalise, and expand our service.
  • To communicate updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Document Data

Your document content is processed in real-time to apply formatting. We do not store the content of your documents on our servers after processing is complete. Temporary processing data is deleted immediately after the formatting job finishes.

4. Google API Scopes

FormatX requests only the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • openid – to verify your identity.
  • email – to identify your account.
  • profile – to display your name and avatar.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents – to read and write the specific document you submit for formatting.

We comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting providers (e.g., Render) that help us operate FormatX, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • AI Providers: We may send document content to AI APIs (e.g., Google Gemini) solely for formatting purposes. These providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information (name, email) as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us, after which all personal data is erased within 30 days.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure OAuth token handling, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

FormatX is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
