The Problem We Solve

Every time you paste AI-generated or web content into Google Docs or Word, formatting breaks. Headings disappear. Code becomes unreadable. References lose structure.

Amarika fixes this instantly. Our AI understands document structure and applies professional formatting in one click — whether you need IEEE citations, corporate report styling, or custom templates.

No more manual reformatting. No more broken layouts. Just clean, publication-ready documents.
