smart logistics and last-mile delivery optimization proposal for freshbasket hyperlocal

so we've been discussing with freshbasket about their delivery operations and honestly the inefficiencies are costing them a fortune every single day

the thing is they're doing around 25000 deliveries daily across bangalore hyderabad and chennai but their delivery cost per order is ₹78 which is way higher than competitors like zepto and blinkit who operate at ₹45-50. their operations head suresh told me that drivers are often crisscrossing the same areas multiple times because there's no intelligent route planning. they use a basic excel-based dispatch system where someone manually assigns orders to riders every morning based on gut feeling. during peak hours like 7-9pm they have 40% order delays and customer complaints go through the roof

problems they mentioned:
- they're burning ₹12 lakhs daily on fuel and rider payments but utilization is only 60%
- no real-time tracking means customers keep calling asking "where is my order" and their call center handles 3000 such calls daily
- rider attrition is 35% monthly because they don't optimize routes for fair workload distribution, some riders get exhausted while others sit idle
- their cold chain is breaking during summer months because nobody tracks temperature during transit
- they're expanding to pune and kochi next quarter but current system can't scale at all
- failed deliveries (customer not home, wrong address) cost them ₹18 per attempt and they have 8% failure rate
- no visibility into which dark stores are performing well and which need more inventory

what we want to propose

so looking at their scale and problems i'm thinking we implement our logistics optimization platform in 3 phases. first phase is integration and visibility where we connect to their order management system and dark store inventory system to get real-time data flowing. we'll deploy GPS trackers on all 1200 delivery bikes and implement our mobile app for riders.

phase 2 is the AI optimization engine. this is the core piece where our algorithms do intelligent batching of orders so one rider picks up 4-5 orders going in same direction. dynamic route optimization that recalculates every 5 minutes based on traffic conditions using google maps api. smart dispatch that considers rider location, vehicle capacity, delivery time windows, and even whether the building has elevator access. predictive ETAs that are actually accurate unlike their current system which lies to customers.

phase 3 is analytics and continuous improvement. we'll build dashboards showing delivery performance by rider, route, dark store, time slot. predictive models for demand forecasting so they can pre-position inventory. temperature monitoring integration for cold chain compliance. customer communication automation with live tracking links.

what they get at the end of all this

delivery cost reduction from ₹78 to ₹52 per order within 6 months
rider utilization improvement from 60% to 82%
live tracking for every order with accurate ETA shared via whatsapp
intelligent order batching that groups 4-5 orders per trip vs current 2-3
dynamic rerouting when traffic conditions change or new urgent orders come
failed delivery reduction from 8% to 3% through smart scheduling and customer pre-alerts
rider app with turn-by-turn navigation and earnings transparency
cold chain monitoring with alerts when temperature exceeds threshold
dark store performance dashboard with inventory optimization recommendations
scalable platform that can handle 100000 deliveries daily for their expansion plans
