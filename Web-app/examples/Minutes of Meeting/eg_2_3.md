meeting - fy26 budget planning and cost optimization discussion

date: december 8 2025
time: 3:00 pm to 5:45 pm
location: finance conference room, 2nd floor noida sector 62 office
organized by: priyanka mehta, cfo

who was present

priyanka mehta - cfo (chair)
sanjay agarwal - ceo
vivek sharma - head of procurement
rekha singh - head of facilities
amit kumar - it infrastructure manager
pooja verma - hr cost controller
manoj tiwari - head of marketing
dinesh jain - fp&a manager

who couldn't make it

rohit kapoor - legal head (traveling for client meeting)

topics we covered

- fy25 actual vs budget analysis
- major cost overruns root cause
- fy26 budget framework and targets
- department wise cost reduction proposals
- capex vs opex strategy
- vendor renegotiation opportunities
- headcount budget allocation
- contingency reserve policy

priyanka opened by saying that fy25 we exceeded budget by 8.7% mainly due to three reasons - unplanned hiring for the bfsi project, cloud infrastructure costs going 40% over due to that data migration mess in august, and the legal settlement with former employee that cost us 85 lakhs. sanjay emphasized that fy26 we absolutely cannot exceed budget because the board has been clear about improving operating margins from current 16% to 19%.

vivek presented procurement analysis. he found that we are paying 15-20% higher than market rates for office supplies because contracts haven't been renegotiated in 3 years. also our capex equipment maintenance contracts are expensive because we're buying annual coverage we don't fully use. he proposed consolidating all vendors and running a proper rfp process which could save 80 lakhs annually.

rekha gave facilities cost breakdown. bangalore office rent renewal is coming up in march and landlord wants 18% increase. she suggested we could negotiate down to 10% if we commit to 5 year lease instead of current 3 year. also proposed reducing leased space in noida office by 4000 sqft since we have hybrid work now and occupancy is only 45% on most days. this could save 28 lakhs per year.

amit discussed it costs. he admitted cloud costs are out of control because developers spin up resources and forget to shut them down. he proposed implementing automated cost monitoring using aws cost explorer and setting up budget alerts. also suggested moving some workloads from aws to on-premise servers we already own. estimated savings of 1.2 crores if implemented properly.

pooja talked about hr costs. biggest cost is obviously salaries which cant be cut but she suggested reducing recruitment agency usage by building internal talent acquisition team. currently we pay 8.33% of ctc to agencies for every hire. if we reduce agency hires from 60% to 30% we save approximately 45 lakhs. also proposed standardizing training budgets across departments because currently some teams spend 3x more than others for similar roles.

manoj said marketing budget should actually increase not decrease because fy26 we are launching 2 new products and need brand awareness. he asked for 15% increase. priyanka pushed back and said he needs to find 10% savings within current budget and she'll consider giving 5% increase only if he shows roi on past campaigns first.

what we decided

1. approved - procurement vendor consolidation project with target savings of 80 lakhs, vivek to lead
2. agreed - bangalore office lease to be negotiated for 5 year term at max 10% increase
3. decided - noida office space reduction of 4000 sqft effective april 2026
4. approved - cloud cost governance program with amit as owner, target 1 crore savings
5. resolved - recruitment agency usage to be capped at 30% of all hires from q1 fy26
6. approved - internal talent acquisition team budget of 35 lakhs for 4 member team
7. deferred - marketing budget increase decision pending roi analysis presentation
8. agreed - all departments must submit fy26 budgets by december 20 with minimum 5% reduction from fy25

action points

priyanka mehta: finalize fy26 budget template and circulate to all by dec 10
vivek sharma: prepare vendor consolidation rfp by dec 15 and identify top 5 high value contracts
rekha singh: initiate bangalore lease negotiation and report back by dec 18
amit kumar: implement aws cost monitoring dashboards and alerts by dec 12
pooja verma: draft internal ta team job descriptions and structure by dec 14
manoj tiwari: prepare marketing roi analysis for fy25 campaigns by dec 20
dinesh jain: consolidate all department budgets and prepare variance analysis by dec 22

risks flagged in the meeting

- landlord might not agree to our lease terms and we may need to consider relocation
- cloud cost reduction might impact application performance if not done carefully
- reducing agency hiring might slow down hiring velocity when we need to scale fast
- department heads might resist 5% mandatory budget cuts especially for revenue teams
